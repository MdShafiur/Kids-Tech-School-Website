Group Visits


Group visit with official letter head may enjoy discounts.

You are advised to confirm your booking at least one week before the visit

Minimum number of children is 10. Please note that groups of more than 20 will be divided into 2-3 entry times, depending on the size of group.

Applicable on weekdays & School Holidays but not on Public Holidays & weekends. Bookings are first come first serve basis.

Full payment must be made 1 week prior to the visit; no refund will be entertained. KidzTechCentre will follow-up upon receiving the confirmation of your visit. Bookings with late payment will be rescheduled.

Please provide a letter with an official letterhead alongside with the details below:

  • Contact Person and Contact Number
  • Name of Group
  • Date of Visit and Preferred Entry Time
  • No. of Visitor (Please specify Adult, Child or Infant)

No outside food & drinks allowed.
Please also bring your own socks. Socks also available for purchase at RM3/pair.
